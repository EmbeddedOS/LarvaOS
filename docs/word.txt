Word (computer architecture)

+ In computer, a word is the nature unit of data used by a particular processor design. \
    A word is a fixed-sized datum handled as a unit by the instruction set or the hardware of processor.

+ The number of bits or digits in a word (the word size, word width, or word length) \
    is an important characteristic of any specific processor design or computer architecture.

+ The size of a word is reflected in many aspects of a computer's structure and operation
